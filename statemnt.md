Category 2: Skill Development
AI-Powered Career Guidance & Upskilling Development Systems

Problem Description
Develop an AI-powered skill and employability platform that can identify skill gaps, recommend relevant training, and connect people to real job opportunities in their region.

Feature Expectations
Solutions should strengthen Skill India, Digital India, and employment missions by:

Mapping workforce skills to industry demand using live job and labor market data
Helping governments and NGOs track training outcomes, placements, and skill gaps
Guiding youth toward in-demand careers instead of random courses
Supporting blue-collar and informal workers with business skills and digital tools
Reducing unemployment and underemployment through data-driven skill development
Additional solution components may include:

AI-based career guidance and skill recommendation systems
Skill gap analysis with personalized learning roadmaps
Resume analysis and improvement tools
AI-powered assessments or mock interview platforms
Vocational and non-technical skill training solutions
